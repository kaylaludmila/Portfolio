About Me


I am, first and foremost, a dog mommy. But one of my many passions outside of my pups, Bentley and Juniper, is art directing. I am a laid-back person who cares about details, never settles, and is never satisfied. I spend my spare time and energy organizing, cooking and living outdoors. Being a creative person, I am constantly finding new ways to learn. I am an adventure-loving world traveler, board game extraordinaire who is hungry for work, eager to learn, and passionate about my commitments.
